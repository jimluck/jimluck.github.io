My Hobby: Serving the Lord

What is My Hobby?

My hobby is serving the Lord Jesus through various ministries at my local church, MAEL. This includes leading intercession, worship, and praise, as well as managing the technical aspects of our worship services.

This service is not only a duty but a joyous expression of my love for God and my community.

Who Am I?

I am a devoted follower of Jesus Christ, deeply involved in my local church, MAEL, where I serve in multiple capacities to support our congregation's spiritual journey.

My roles vary from leading worship to handling the technical setup for our services, each role offering a unique way to glorify God.

When Do I Serve?

My service is ongoing, with special emphasis on weekly worship services, prayer meetings, and any time our community comes together to celebrate and worship God.

Each moment of service is a blessed opportunity to deepen my relationship with God and my community.

Where Do I Serve?

My service is primarily located at MAEL, my local church. This sacred space is where we gather to connect with God and each other.

However, through digital technology, we extend our reach globally via online broadcasts of our services.

How Do I Serve?

I serve by leading the musical aspect of worship and managing the technical operations like sound engineering, live streaming, and video production to ensure our message reaches far and wide.

My service is a blend of artistic expression and technical proficiency, all dedicated to glorifying God.

Why Do I Serve?

My service is an expression of my deepest love for God and my desire to follow His will by loving my neighbors as myself. It's a tangible manifestation of my faith and commitment.

Through this service, I seek to inspire others to come closer to God and find their own path of service.
